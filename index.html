Nearby Attractions

Udawalawe National Park

28 km - 30 minutes drive

Home to over 500 elephants with guaranteed sightings. One of Sri Lanka's premier wildlife destinations featuring water buffalo, crocodiles, spotted deer, and 184 bird species. Less crowded than other parks, perfect for families and first-time safari guests.

Elephant Safaris Year-round Viewing Morning & Evening Tours

Elephant Transit Home

30 km - 35 minutes drive

The world's only facility caring for orphaned baby elephants before reintegration into wild herds. Witness feeding times from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. A unique conservation experience complementing your safari adventure.

Chandrika Lake

On-site at resort

One of the largest reservoirs in Sri Lanka, perfect for swimming, picnicking, sunset viewing, fishing, and seasonal boating. The lake creates a serene atmosphere for relaxation after your safari adventures.

Sankapala Temple

15 km - 20 minutes drive

Ancient Buddhist temple from 161-131 BCE with 14 rock caves, historical inscriptions, and panoramic mountain views. Built by one of King Dutugemunu's legendary warriors, offering authentic cultural heritage experiences.

Ridiyagama Safari Park

40 km - 45 minutes drive

Sri Lanka's first man-made safari park featuring African lions, Bengal tigers, zebras, and giraffes in a drive-through format. Perfect for families with children seeking diverse wildlife experiences.

Why Our Location is Perfect

Main Road Access

Located on New Town Road with direct connection to Ratnapura-Embilipitiya highway. Easy 3-4 hour drive from Colombo via A4 highway. Well-signposted and GPS-friendly location.

Public Transport

Central Embilipitiya bus station only 1.4 km away. Regular buses from Colombo, Ratnapura, and Hambantota. Tuk-tuks and taxi services readily available.

Strategic Central Location

In Embilipitiya town, not isolated. Shops, restaurants, banks, ATMs, and medical facilities within walking distance. Safe parking available at resort.

Safari Convenience

Just 30 minutes to Udawalawe means later wake-up times for morning safaris. Return to resort for breakfast. Multiple safaris possible in one day with comfortable scheduling.
